📝 Teacher Notes - P94 igh Sound

S1 Title: Introduce the lesson theme - learning igh sound.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show igh pattern. Emphasize the /aɪ/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S7 Words: Introduce 4 words with igh. Point to the pictures and say the words clearly.

S8 Blending: Demonstrate how to blend. Point to each part as you say it.

S9 Listen & Tap: Students listen and tap the correct picture. Auto-plays audio and advances.

S10 Story: Read the short story. Emphasize words with igh.

S11 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S12 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
