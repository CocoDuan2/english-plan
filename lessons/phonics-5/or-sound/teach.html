📝 Teacher Notes - P83 or Sound

S1 Title: Introduce the or sound lesson. "Today Peppa is at the farm with a horse! Let's learn the or sound!"

S2 Hello: Peppa greets students and introduces the or sound.

S3 Sound + Mouth: Show or. Emphasize /ɔːr/ sound. Model: make a round mouth for /ɔː/ then curl tongue for /r/. Compare with ar — "ar opens wide, or makes a round shape!" Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace the letters o and r. Check validates the path through key points.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words with or. Highlight the or part in pink. Different characters present each word for variety. Ask: "What sound do you hear?"

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Show how or stays together: f-or-k → fork, h-or-se → horse. The or is a team!

S10 Listen & Tap: Interactive quiz. Audio plays automatically. Students listen and tap the correct picture. 4 rounds. Encourage: "Listen carefully!"

S11 Story: Read the story. Emphasize or words. Ask: "What does Peppa ride?" "What does George find?"

S12 Song: Sing the or song together. Clap on each or word. Make it fun!

S13 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say them.

S14 Bye: Celebrate! Preview next lesson (er sound).
