📝 Teacher Notes - P96 y as /iː/

S1 Title: Introduce y as /iː/ sound at word endings.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Explain that y at the end of words says /iː/. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace letter y with finger/mouse.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words ending with y. Emphasize the /iː/ sound.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Tap: Interactive game - students listen and tap the correct picture.

S11 Story: Read the story. Emphasize words ending with y.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say them.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
