📝 Teacher Notes - P97 Soft c

S1 Title: Introduce soft c - when c sounds like /s/.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Explain soft c rule: before e, i, y → /s/. Have students repeat.

S4-S7 Words: Introduce 4 words with soft c. Point to pictures and emphasize the /s/ sound.

S8 Listen & Tap: Interactive game - students listen and tap the correct picture.

S9 Story: Read the story. Emphasize soft c words.

S10 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say them.

S11 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson.
