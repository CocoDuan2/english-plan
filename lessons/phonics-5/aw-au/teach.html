📝 Teacher Notes - P91 aw/au

S1 Title: Introduce aw/au as a vowel pair that makes the same /ɔː/ sound.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and explains we're learning TWO spellings today.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show both aw and au side by side. Emphasize they both say /ɔː/. Have students repeat 3 times. Show the mouth animation — open wide, round lips. Explain: "aw" is usually at the end (saw, paw, draw), "au" is usually in the middle (sauce, because).

S4 Trace: Students trace "aw". Focus on the round 'a' and the zigzag 'w'.

S5-S8 Words: Three "aw" words (saw, paw, draw) and one "au" word (sauce). Point out which spelling each word uses.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Keep aw/au together as one sound unit. For "draw": d-r-aw. For "sauce": s-au-ce.

S10 Listen & Tap: Students listen and tap the correct picture. Auto-plays audio with distractors from other lessons.

S11 Story: Read the short story, emphasizing /ɔː/ words. Ask students to clap when they hear the sound.

S12 Game: Drawing Easel — tap each picture to "draw" it. The picture appears with a drawing animation effect.

S13 Song: Sing the aw/au chant. Tap each line to hear it. Encourage students to clap along.

S14 All Together: Click any picture to hear it. Review all 4 words.

S15 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson (ew).
