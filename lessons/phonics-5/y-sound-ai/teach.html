📝 Teacher Notes - P95 Y Sound /aɪ/

S1 Title: Introduce Y sound /aɪ/ theme.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets students.

S3 Sound: Show Y and /aɪ/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S7 Words: Introduce 4 words with Y sound. Point and say clearly.

S8 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Point to each letter.

S9 Listen & Tap: Students listen and tap correct picture. Auto-plays audio.

S10 Story: Read story. Emphasize Y words.

S11 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly.

S12 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson.
