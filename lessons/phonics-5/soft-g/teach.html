📝 Teacher Notes - P98 Soft g

S1 Title: Introduce soft g - when g sounds like /dʒ/ before e, i, y.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show letter g. Emphasize the /dʒ/ sound (like j). Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace letter g with finger/mouse. Check button validates the path.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words with soft g. Point to the pictures and say the words clearly.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate how to blend letters. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Tap: Students listen to a word and tap the correct picture. Auto-plays next word after correct answer.

S11 Story: Read the short story. Emphasize words with soft g.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
