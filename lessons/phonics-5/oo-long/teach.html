📝 Teacher Notes - P88 oo (long)

S1 Title: Introduce the long oo sound.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets students.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show oo and emphasize /uː/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace oo with finger/mouse.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words with long oo sound.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Note oo stays together.

S10 Listen & Tap: Students listen and tap the correct picture.

S11 Story: Read the story emphasizing oo words.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson.
