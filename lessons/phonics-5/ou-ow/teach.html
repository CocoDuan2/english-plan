📝 Teacher Notes - P89 ou/ow

S1 Title: Introduce ou/ow as a diphthong pair that makes the same sound.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and explains we're learning TWO spellings today.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show both ou and ow side by side. Emphasize they both say /aʊ/. Have students repeat 3 times. Explain: "ou" is usually in the middle (house, mouse), "ow" can be at the end (cow, now).

S4 Trace: Students trace "ou". Focus on the round shape of 'o' and the 'u' shape.

S5-S8 Words: Two "ou" words (house, mouse) and two "ow" words (cow, now). Point out which spelling each word uses.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Keep ou/ow together as one sound unit.

S10 Listen & Tap: Students listen and tap the correct picture. Auto-plays audio, includes distractors from other lessons.

S11 Story: Read the short story, emphasizing /aʊ/ words.

S12 All Together: Click any picture to hear it. Review all 4 words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson (oi/oy).
