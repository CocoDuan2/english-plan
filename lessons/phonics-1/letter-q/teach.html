📝 Teacher Notes - P17 Letter Q

S1 Title: Introduce letter Q - Peppa dresses as a queen.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets students.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show Q q. Emphasize /kw/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace letter Q (circle + tail).

S5-S8 Words: Introduce queen, quilt, question, quick.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Point to each letter.

S10 Listen & Say: Students click to hear words and repeat.

S11 Story: Read the story. Emphasize Q words.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate and preview letter R.
