📝 Teacher Notes - P2 Letter B

S1 Title: Introduce the lesson theme - learning letter B.

S2 Hello: George greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show uppercase and lowercase B. Emphasize the /b/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace letter B with finger/mouse. Check button validates the path.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words starting with B. Point to the pictures and say the words clearly.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate how to blend letters. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Say: Students click buttons to hear words. Encourage them to repeat.

S11 Story: Read the short story. Emphasize words with letter B.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
