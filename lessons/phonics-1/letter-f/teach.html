📝 Teacher Notes - P6 Letter F

S1 Title: Introduce the lesson theme - Peppa goes fishing, learning letter F.

S2 Hello: Daddy Pig greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show uppercase and lowercase F. Emphasize the /f/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace letter F (3 strokes: vertical + top bar + middle bar). Check button validates the path.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words starting with F: fish, frog, flower, fan.

S9 Blending: Choose a picture, watch letters slide in one by one, then the full word pops.

S10 Listen & Say: Students click buttons to hear words. Encourage them to repeat.

S11 Listen & Tap: Hear a word and tap the matching picture. 3 choices each round.

S12 Fishing Game: Tap on each floating item in the pond to hear its name and catch it.

S13 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say them.

S14 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview letter G.
