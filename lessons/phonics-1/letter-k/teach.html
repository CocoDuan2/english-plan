📝 Teacher Notes - P11 Letter K

S1 Title: Introduce letter K - Peppa flies a kite.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show K k. Emphasize /k/ sound. Repeat 3 times.

S4 Trace: Students trace letter K with finger/mouse.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce kite, king, key, kid. Point and say clearly.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Point to each letter.

S10 Listen & Say: Students click to hear words. Encourage repetition.

S11 Story: Read the story. Emphasize K words.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly.

S13 Bye: Celebrate and preview letter L.
