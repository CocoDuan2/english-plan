🎨 Day 1 · Colors
🧑‍🏫 Say: "Hi! Today we learn 5 colors! Are you ready?"
🧑‍🏫 Tap each grey picture to reveal the color! Say: "RED! This is RED! Can you say RED?" Child can tap again to practice!
🧑‍🏫 Tap each grey picture to paint it! Say: "The apple is RED!"
🧑‍🏫 Say the color, child finds it! "Where is RED?"
🧑‍🏫 Flip two cards! Say the color each time. Match = "Yes! Same color!"
