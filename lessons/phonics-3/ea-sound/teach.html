📝 Teacher Notes - P49 ea Sound

S1 Title: Introduce the ea sound lesson.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets students.

S3 Sound: Show ea and emphasize /iː/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S7 Words: Introduce 4 words with ea. Point to pictures and say words clearly.

S8 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Point to each part as you say it.

S9 Listen & Tap: Students listen and tap the correct picture. Game auto-plays.

S10 Story: Read the story. Emphasize ea words.

S11 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say them.

S12 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson.
