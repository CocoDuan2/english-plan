📝 Teacher Notes - P47 ai/ay

S1 Title: Introduce the lesson - learning ai/ay digraphs.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Sound: Show ai and ay. Emphasize the /eɪ/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S7 Words: Introduce 4 words with ai/ay. Point to pictures and say words clearly.

S8 Listen & Tap: Interactive game - students listen and tap the correct picture.

S9 Story: Read the short story. Emphasize words with ai/ay.

S10 All Together: Review all 4 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S11 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
