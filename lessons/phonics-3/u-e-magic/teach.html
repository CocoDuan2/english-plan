📝 Teacher Notes - P45 Magic E: u_e

S1 Title: Fourth Magic E lesson! Review a_e, i_e, o_e briefly. Today: u_e says /juː/.

S2 Hello: Peppa says Magic E works on U too! Key: U says its name "YOU" /juː/.

S3 Magic E Rule: Show contrast: "cub" → "cube". Explain: U normally says /ʌ/ (short), but with Magic E, U says /juː/ (its name!). Have students repeat both sounds.

S4 How Magic E Works: Interactive demo with "cute". Click to add E and watch magic. Repeat a few times.

S5 Trace: Students trace lowercase 'u' (two vertical lines with rounded bottom) and 'e'. Switch with tabs.

S6 cute: Use a cute puppy emoji. Emphasize: c-u-t-e, the U says /juː/.

S7 tube: Toothpaste/tube emoji. Have students say each letter clearly.

S8 mule: Donkey emoji (closest to mule). Fun animal word!

S9 flute: Musical instrument. Peppa loves music!

S10 June: Calendar month. Capital J because it's a proper noun.

S11 Blending: Pick any word to see letter-by-letter blending. The 'e' is silent but makes U long!

S12 Listen & Tap: Auto-plays word audio, students tap correct picture from 4 choices. 🔊 Again button to replay.

S13 Song: Fun u_e song. Sing with actions!

S14 All Together: Review all 5 words. Point randomly and ask students.

S15 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson (e_e).
