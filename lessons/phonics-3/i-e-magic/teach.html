📝 Teacher Notes - P43 Magic E: i_e

S1 Title: Second Magic E lesson! Remind students about a_e from last time.

S2 Hello: Peppa says Magic E works on other vowels too! Build excitement.

S3 Magic E Rule: Show contrast: "kit" → "kite". Explain: I normally says /ɪ/ (short), but with Magic E, I says /aɪ/ (its name!). Have students repeat both sounds.

S4 How Magic E Works: Interactive demo with "bike". Click to add E and watch it transform. Repeat a few times.

S5 Trace: Students trace lowercase 'i' (line + dot) and 'e'. Switch with tabs.

S6-S10 Words: 5 i_e words. Highlight the pattern: the 'i' is pink, 'e' is light pink. Have students say each word clearly.

S11 Blending: Pick any word to see letter-by-letter blending animation. Remind: the 'e' is silent!

S12 Listen & Tap: Audio plays a word, students tap the correct picture from 3 choices. Great for listening practice!

S13 Song: Fun i_e song. Sing together! Emphasize the /aɪ/ sound.

S14 All Together: Review all 5 words. Click any to hear pronunciation. Point randomly.

S15 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson (o_e).
