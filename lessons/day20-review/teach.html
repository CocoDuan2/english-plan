Teacher Notes - Day 20 Review

Slide 1-2: Welcome to Adventure Park! Four stations = four themes (Day 16-19).

Slides 3-6: Review each station: Colors (It's ___!), Animals (I see a ___!), Body (This is my ___!), Food (I like ___!). Point and ask students to say words + sentences.

Slide 7: Ticket Booth - Click each ticket to reveal the sentence pattern. Practice all four patterns.

Slide 8: All Together - 20 words grid. Point randomly and ask: "What's this?" Students respond with word + sentence.

Slide 9: Celebration! Great job reviewing 20 words and 4 sentence patterns!

Keyboard: ← → arrows to navigate, T to toggle notes.
