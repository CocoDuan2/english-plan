👃 Day 3 · Body
🧑‍🏫 Say: "Hi! Today we learn about our body! Touch your head! Ready?"
🧑‍🏫 Review colors & animals! Say: "Find the RED one!" or "Find the CAT!" Child taps the right one.
🧑‍🏫 Tap each grey body part to reveal it! Say: "This is your HEAD! Touch your HEAD!"
🧑‍🏫 Say: "Touch your NOSE!" Child taps the right body part on the face!
🧑‍🏫 Read the prompt, child finds the matching body part!
🧑‍🏫 Flip two cards! Say the body part each time. Match = "Yes! Same! EYES!"
