📝 Teacher Notes - P29 -ap Family

S1 Title: Introduce the -ap word family theme.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Family Pattern: Show -ap pattern. Emphasize the /æp/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S8 Words: Introduce 5 words in -ap family. Show how changing the first letter makes new words.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate CVC blending. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Tap: NEW interactive game! Audio plays automatically. Students tap the correct picture. Correct answer shows ✅ + confetti, then auto-advances. Wrong answer shows ❌ and allows retry.

S11 Story: Read the short story about Peppa's nap time. Emphasize -ap words.

S12 All Together: Review all 5 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
