📝 Teacher Notes - P30 -ad Family

S1 Title: Introduce the -ad word family theme with Daddy Pig.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Family Pattern: Show -ad pattern. Emphasize the /æd/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S8 Words: Introduce 5 words in -ad family. Show how changing the first letter makes new words.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate CVC blending. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Tap: Interactive game! Audio plays automatically. Students tap the correct picture. Correct answer shows ✅ + confetti, then auto-advances. Wrong answer shows ❌ and allows retry.

S11 Story: Read the short story about Peppa and Daddy. Emphasize -ad words.

S12 All Together: Review all 5 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
