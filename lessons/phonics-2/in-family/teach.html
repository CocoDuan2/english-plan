📝 Teacher Notes - P35 -in Family

S1 Title: Introduce the -in word family theme.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Family & Sound: Show -in family. Emphasize /ɪn/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S8 Words: Introduce 5 words: pin, win, bin, fin, tin. Point to pictures and say words clearly.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Tap: Audio plays automatically. Students tap the correct picture. Answers are validated.

S11 Story: Read the story. Emphasize -in words.

S12 All Together: Review all 5 words. Point randomly and ask students to say them.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
