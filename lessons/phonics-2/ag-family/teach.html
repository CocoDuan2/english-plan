📝 Teacher Notes - P31 -ag Family

S1 Title: Introduce the -ag word family theme.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets and invites students to learn.

S3 Family Pattern: Show -ag pattern. Emphasize /æg/ sound. Have students repeat 3 times.

S4-S8 Words: Introduce 5 words: bag, tag, wag, flag, drag. Point to pictures and say words clearly.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate CVC blending. Point to each letter as you say it.

S10 Listen & Tap: Interactive quiz - students listen and tap the correct picture.

S11 Story: Read the short story. Emphasize -ag words.

S12 All Together: Review all 5 words. Point randomly and ask students to say the words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate completion and preview next lesson.
