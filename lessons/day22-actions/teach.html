Teacher Notes - Day 22

Story: Sports Day! Mummy Pig is the referee.

Sentence: I can ___!

Yesterday: Quick review of one/two/three from Day 21.

Whistle Reveal: Tap each whistle to reveal the action. Demonstrate each action with students.

Race Track: Point randomly and have students say "I can run!" etc. Encourage them to do the action!

Tips: This is a high-energy lesson. Get students moving! Do the actions together.
