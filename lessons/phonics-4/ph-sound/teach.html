📝 Teacher Notes - P76 ph Sound

S1 Title: Introduce ph digraph lesson.

S2 Hello: Peppa greets students.

S3 Letter & Sound: Show ph makes /f/ sound. Have students repeat.

S4 Trace: Students trace ph with finger/mouse.

S5-S8 Words: Introduce 4 words with ph. Point and say clearly.

S9 Blending: Demonstrate blending letters into words.

S10 Listen & Tap: Interactive quiz - students listen and tap correct picture.

S11 Story: Read story emphasizing ph words.

S12 All Together: Review all 4 words.

S13 Bye: Celebrate and preview next lesson.
