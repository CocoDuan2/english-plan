Teacher Notes

Slide 1 (Title): "Today we're going on a treasure hunt! We'll find numbers!"

Slide 2 (Hello): "George and Peppa are ready! Let's find treasures together!"

Slide 3 (Yesterday): "Quick review! Point and say: apple, milk, egg. I like apple!"

Slides 4-8 (Numbers): Each number shows treasure items. Count together: "One star! Two hearts! Three flowers! Four butterflies! Five balloons!" Use fingers to count.

Slide 9 (Chests): Click each chest to reveal. "Let's open the chests! What's inside? One! Two! Three!"

Slide 10 (Map): Point randomly. "How many? One! Two! Three! Four! Five!" Encourage counting with fingers.

Slide 11 (Bye): "We found all the treasures! Great job! See you next time!"

Sentence Practice: "I have one! I have two!" Repeat with gestures.
